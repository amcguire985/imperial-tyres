Who We Are

About Imperial Products

Established in 2008, Imperial Products Limited is a leading player in the Nigerian tyre industry with over a decade of experience serving the market.

We understand the unique requirements and challenges of Nigerian road conditions, catering to a wide range of customers - from individual dealers to major institutions in the construction and transportation sectors.

Through exclusive partnerships with leading manufacturers worldwide, we deliver an extensive selection of tyres for trucks, passenger cars, off-road vehicles, agriculture, and industrial applications.

Our Mission

To provide top-quality tyres and exceptional service meeting the diverse needs of our customers in Nigeria, delivering reliable, durable, and performance-driven solutions that exceed industry standards.

Our Vision

To be the tyre industry leader in Nigeria, recognised for our unwavering commitment to excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction, while expanding into retail and distribution.
